Privacy Policy

Sand Sort Lab

This policy explains how Sand Sort Lab handles information when you play the game. The current version is designed as an offline puzzle experience and does not collect personal information from players.

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Current data posture No account, no ads, no analytics SDK, local progress only.

No Account

You can play without creating an account or providing a name, email address, phone number, or login credential.

No Tracking

The current app build does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or cross-app tracking SDKs.

On-Device Saves

Coins, lives, level progress, settings, and similar gameplay state are stored locally on your device.

Information We Collect

Sand Sort Lab does not collect personal information in the current version. We do not ask for contact information, precise location, photos, contacts, microphone access, camera access, or payment information.

Gameplay progress and preferences may be saved locally on your device so the game can remember your levels, coins, lives, sound settings, and similar in-game state. This local data is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

The current version does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, social login, cloud save, or payment SDKs. If those services are added later, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

How Information Is Used

Local gameplay data is used only to provide app functionality, such as resuming progress, restoring game settings, displaying coin and life balances, and continuing unlocked levels.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share player personal information. Because the current version does not transmit personal information to us, there is no player personal information for us to share with third parties.

Data Retention And Deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you delete the app, clear the app data, or the app overwrites it during normal gameplay. Deleting the app removes locally stored game progress according to your device operating system behavior.

Children's Privacy

Sand Sort Lab is a general puzzle game and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If a future version adds any feature that collects personal information, this policy will be updated and appropriate consent flows will be added where required.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy when the game changes. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the developer through the support contact listed on the App Store product page for Sand Sort Lab. If you voluntarily provide contact information when making a request, it will be used only to respond to that request.
